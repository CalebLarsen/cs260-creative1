- A while back we started making the Go language. Unfortunately, we have decided to discontinue it since Unum is clearly superior. If this affects you in any way, know that the future is bright because Unum will play a role in it.

- All official Church applications will now be rewritten using Unum. We expect that this will activity levels by at least 300%. We appreciate and thank Caleb for his clearly inspired work with Unum.
