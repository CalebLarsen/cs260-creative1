Contribute
There are many ways that you can help the cause. Any and all efforts are appreciated. Our most urgent needs are the following:

  • Buy me pizza. I get hungry every single day, but you can help fight that. Also if anyone knows a cure to DRH (daily recurring hunger) please code up a solution and we will merge it into Unum.

  • Send me money. I really like money and it turns out that its useful for tons of things. However, the most likely outcome is for your donation to be a more roundabout way of buying me pizza. Some people may find this easier.

  • Spread the word. Studies show that at least 5% of the world still has not heard about Unum and we need your help to find them and either preach to them, or exterminate them.

  • Write Unum. One of the most helpful things would be if you could write Unum, travel back in time, give it to me, and then let me take all the credit. We only really need one person to do it, but if there are multiple entries, we will compare quality of implementations.
